About HackOR

12 Schools. 600 Hackors. 48 hours of Hacking.

This spring in 2021, we—students from the major schools and universities of Oregon—are welcoming students from all over the world to join us from March 26 - 28th at the HackOR hackathon: a student event for all to enjoy a virtual weekend of programming, workshops, mentors, networking, and other fun activities.

We are very beginner friendly and encourage all students interested to join! We will be offering all hackors workshops, panels, and mentors to guide you through the process. You can enter with a team, or form one through our gathertown events and make some new friends! Join us by registering now.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a project-based coding competition over the span of a few days. During the competition, participants team up in groups of four to create a tech solution, which is then presented to a set of judges.

With workshops, mentors, and collaborative teamwork, hackathons are great ways to build your skills in tech, design, and ideation.

At HackOR 2021, we provide 30+ hours worth of workshops and events, along with opportunities to collaborate with other students passionate about technical learning!

How do I get started?

Applications for HackOR 2021 is closed, but you can stay tuned for future events, opportunities, and updates by following our social media. Thanks everyone for coming and see you next year!

For more information regarding HackOR attendees and teams, please check out our FAQ.
